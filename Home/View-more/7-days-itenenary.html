Day 1: Arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport

As you walk out of Kilimanjaro International Airport, your personal Tanzania Specialist guide greets you with a warm welcome: "Karibu Tanzania - welcome to Tanzania!" The guide takes you to your hotel to relax and enjoy the African atmosphere.

Day 2: Lake Manyara National Park

Explore the diverse landscapes of Lake Manyara National Park, famous for its tree-climbing lions, flocks of flamingos, and lush forests. It's a perfect start to your Tanzanian adventure.

Day 3: Serengeti National Park (Central West/East)

Serengeti National Park is renowned for its abundant wildlife and the Great Migration. This day is filled with thrilling game drives where you may spot lions, elephants, and countless other species in their natural habitat.

Day 4: Serengeti National Park (Central West/East)

Continue exploring Serengeti’s expansive plains, tracking wildlife and enjoying the serene landscapes. Another exciting day to witness the park's diverse ecosystems and rich fauna.

Day 5: Ngorongoro Conservation Area (including Crater)

Explore the Ngorongoro Crater, home to a rich variety of wildlife within its walls, including lions, zebras, buffalos, and rare black rhinos. This natural wonder is a highlight of any Tanzanian safari.

Days 6 - 7: Return Flight from Kilimanjaro International Airport

After an unforgettable safari, relax and reflect on your journey before heading back to Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO) for your flight home.
