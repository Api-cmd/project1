Your Journey

Day 1

Arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport

Welcome to Tanzania! Get ready for your climb as you are transferred to your hotel for preparation and rest.

Day 2

Lemosho-Shira Route (1/6)

Route: Drop off (3,500 m/11,483 ft) - Shira 2 Camp (3,890 m/12,762 ft)

Begin your ascent along the Lemosho route, traveling to Shira 2 Camp with stunning views of the surrounding landscapes.

Day 3

Lemosho-Shira Route (2/6)

Route: Shira 2 Camp (3,890 m/12,762 ft) - Lava Tower (4,630 m/15,190 ft) - Barranco Camp (3,950 m/12,959 ft)

Ascend to Lava Tower and experience stunning high-altitude views before descending to Barranco Camp for an overnight stay.

Day 4

Lemosho-Shira Route (3/6)

Route: Barranco Camp (3,950 m/12,959 ft) - Karanga Camp (4,040 m/13,254 ft)

Tackle the Barranco Wall before trekking to Karanga Camp, where you will rest and acclimatize.

Day 5

Lemosho-Shira Route (4/6)

Route: Karanga Camp (4,040 m/13,254 ft) - Barafu Camp (4,645 m/15,239 ft)

Continue your trek to Barafu Camp, the final stop before your summit push.

Day 6

Lemosho-Shira Route (5/6)

Route: Barafu Camp (4,645 m/15,239 ft) - Uhuru Peak (5,895 m/19,340 ft) - Millennium Camp (3,820 m/12,532 ft)

Summit day! Reach the highest point in Africa, Uhuru Peak, and descend to Millennium Camp for rest.

Day 7

Lemosho-Shira Route (6/6)

Route: Millennium Camp (3,820 m/12,532 ft) - Mweka Gate (1,640 m/5,380 ft) - Hotel

Complete your descent and return to the hotel for celebration and relaxation.

Days 8-9

Flight Home

After the climb, depart from Kilimanjaro International Airport with unforgettable memories of your adventure.
