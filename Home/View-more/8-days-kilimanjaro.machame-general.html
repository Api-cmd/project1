Your Journey

01

Arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport

Prepare for your climb as you relax at your accommodation and get ready for an incredible adventure ahead.

02

Machame Route (1/6)

Machame Gate (1,800 m/5,905 ft) to Machame Camp (2,835 m/9,301 ft). Begin your journey through the rainforest to Machame Camp.

03

Machame Route (2/6)

Machame Camp (2,835 m/9,301 ft) to Shira Camp (3,839 m/12,595 ft). Ascend through moorland to Shira Plateau.

04

Machame Route (3/6)

Shira Camp (3,839 m/12,595 ft) to Lava Tower (4,630 m/15,190 ft), then descend to Barranco Camp (3,950 m/12,959 ft). Acclimatize while exploring stunning terrain.

05

Machame Route (4/6)

Barranco Camp (3,950 m/12,959 ft) to Barafu Camp (4,645 m/15,239 ft). Cross the Barranco Wall and prepare for your summit attempt.

06

Machame Route (5/6)

Barafu Camp (4,645 m/15,239 ft) to Uhuru Peak (5,895 m/19,340 ft), then descend to Millennium Camp (3,820 m/12,532 ft). Reach the summit of Africa!

07

Machame Route (6/6)

Millennium Camp (3,820 m/12,532 ft) to Mweka Gate (1,640 m/5,380 ft), then return to your hotel. Celebrate your achievement!

08-09

Flight Home

Return home from Kilimanjaro International Airport with unforgettable memories of your climb.
