Your Journey

01

Arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport

Welcome to Tanzania! Prepare for your exciting climb ahead as you relax at your accommodation.

02

Marangu Route (1/5)

Marangu Gate (1,860 m/6,102 ft) to Mandara Huts (2,720 m/8,923 ft). Begin your climb through lush rainforest to Mandara Huts.

03

Marangu Route (2/5)

Mandara Huts (2,720 m/8,923 ft) to Horombo Huts (3,720 m/12,204 ft). Trek through moorland to reach Horombo Huts.

04

Marangu Route (3/5)

Horombo Huts (3,720 m/12,204 ft) to Kibo Huts Camp (4,720 m/15,485 ft). Ascend through alpine desert to Kibo Huts.

05

Marangu Route (4/5)

Kibo Huts (4,720 m/15,485 ft) to Uhuru Peak (5,895 m/19,340 ft) and back to Horombo Huts (3,720 m/12,204 ft). Reach the summit and descend.

06

Marangu Route (5/5)

Horombo Huts (3,720 m/12,204 ft) to Marangu Gate (1,860 m/6,102 ft), then to your hotel. Celebrate your achievement!

07-08

Flight Home

Return home from Kilimanjaro International Airport with unforgettable memories of your climb.
