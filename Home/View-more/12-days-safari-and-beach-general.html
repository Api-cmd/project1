Your Journey

01

Arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport

Welcome to Tanzania! Upon arrival, our team will greet you and transfer you to your hotel to prepare for your exciting journey.

02

Tarangire National Park

Discover the beauty of Tarangire National Park, known for its majestic baobab trees and large elephant herds.

03

Ngorongoro Conservation Area (including Crater)

Visit the world-famous Ngorongoro Crater, a natural wonder filled with diverse wildlife and breathtaking scenery.

04

Serengeti National Park (Central West/East)

Embark on an exciting safari in Serengeti National Park, one of Africa's most iconic destinations for wildlife viewing.

05

Northern Serengeti & the Great Migration

Witness the awe-inspiring Great Migration and the possibility of seeing the dramatic Mara River crossing.

06

Northern Serengeti & the Great Migration

Spend another day exploring the Northern Serengeti, capturing unforgettable moments of the Great Migration.

07

Flight to Zanzibar

Enjoy a scenic flight to Zanzibar, where you’ll begin the relaxation phase of your adventure.

08

Zanzibar

Unwind on Zanzibar’s pristine beaches, explore historic Stone Town, or partake in water sports and local excursions.

09

Zanzibar

Continue enjoying the island’s tropical charm with opportunities to relax, snorkel, or explore Zanzibar's rich culture.

10

Zanzibar

Conclude your adventure in Zanzibar before heading back home, carrying unforgettable memories of Tanzania and its treasures.
