Arusha

Walking Safari in Arusha National Park

Experience an unforgettable walking safari lasting 6 to 8 hours through Arusha National Park. Guided by an experienced ranger, get up close with giraffes, buffaloes, zebras, warthogs, and blue monkeys while learning about the park's unique flora and fauna.

Arusha City Tour

Explore the vibrant city of Arusha on a half-day city tour, visiting local markets, schools, and churches while enjoying a drink at a local pub.

Canoeing in Arusha National Park

Enjoy a peaceful canoe safari on one of the lakes in Arusha National Park, listening to the birds and spotting wildlife.

Tanzanian Souvenir Market

Visit a vibrant souvenir market filled with traditional paintings, woodwork, clothes made from African fabrics, and multi-colored jewelry.

Climb Mount Longido

Experience a thrilling climb on Mount Longido, located near the border with Kenya, with the option to camp overnight.

Mountain Bike Tour Outside Arusha

Discover local life on a mountain bike tour through beautiful paths and flat terrain that are relatively easy.

Horseback Riding in Arusha

Go off the beaten track with a horseback safari through the untouched nature surrounding Mount Meru.

Meserani Snake Park & Maasai Museum

Visit a snake park showcasing Tanzania's snake species, followed by a tour of the Maasai Museum.

Tanzanite Museum Arusha

Explore one of the world's rarest gemstones at the Tanzanite Museum and learn about its history and processing.

Visit a Coffee Plantation in Arusha

Discover the coffee culture of the region by visiting one of the many coffee plantations around Moshi and Arusha.

Hike to the Mount Meru Waterfalls

Experience a scenic hike to the breathtaking waterfalls of Mount Meru, the second-highest mountain in Tanzania.

Featured Activities in Ngorongoro

Empakaai Crater Hike

The Empakaai Crater is located about 40 kilometers northeast of the Ngorongoro Crater. This adventurous hike takes about 45 minutes to descend towards the lake at the bottom of the crater, offering stunning views and the chance to spot flamingos in the lake.

Two-day Hike from Ngorongoro to Lake Natron

Embark on a two-day hike from Ngorongoro's Empakaai Crater to the stunning Lake Natron. Guided by local Maasai and a park ranger, you'll pass through remote Maasai villages and spend the night camping in a tent. This trek offers a deep immersion into the beautiful landscapes of northern Tanzania.

Visit Olduvai Gorge

Olduvai Gorge is a must-visit site for history lovers, where some of the earliest human remains were discovered. Located between Ngorongoro Crater and Serengeti National Park, the 100-meter deep ravine is an important archaeological site and provides fascinating insights into human evolution.
