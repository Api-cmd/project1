Day 1: Arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport

As you walk out of Kilimanjaro International Airport, your personal Tanzania Specialist guide greets you with a big smile: "Karibu Tanzania - welcome to Tanzania!" The guide takes you straight to your hotel to relax and take in the African atmosphere. The next day, your exclusive, private safari begins!

Day 2: Tarangire National Park

Known for its large groups of elephants and iconic baobab trees, Tarangire National Park is home to a vibrant ecosystem. The Tarangire River is the only water source in the dry season, attracting up to 250 elephants!

Day 3: Serengeti National Park (Central West/East)

Serengeti National Park is famous for its incredible wildlife and as the prime destination to witness the Great Migration. The stampeding herds of wildebeests are impressive, as are the numerous predators that thrive here: lions, cheetahs, leopards, hyenas, and wild dogs.

Days 4 - 5: Northern Serengeti & the Great Migration

In northern Serengeti, you’ll witness millions of wildebeests and zebras as they attempt the challenging Mara River crossing, an iconic moment of the Great Migration. Watch as they brave the river full of crocodiles and try to evade the big cats waiting on the other side.

Day 6: Serengeti National Park (Central West/East)

Explore more of Serengeti National Park and marvel at the abundant wildlife and endless plains. Enjoy a day filled with thrilling safari experiences in this renowned park.

Day 7: Ngorongoro Conservation Area (incl. Crater)

Discover the famous Ngorongoro Crater, one of Africa's seven natural wonders. Within the crater’s lush ecosystem, you’ll encounter diverse wildlife, including lions, zebras, buffalos, and black rhinos.

Days 8 - 9: Flight Home from Kilimanjaro International Airport

Depending on your flight schedule, relax at your lodge before heading back to Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO) for your journey home.
